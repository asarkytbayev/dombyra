Dombyra

About dombyra

The dombyra is a long-necked Qazaq lute and a musical string instrument. It is a popular instrument among Turkic communities in Central Asian countries such as Qazaqstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Afghanistan, as well as Mongolia. (Source: Wikipedia)
